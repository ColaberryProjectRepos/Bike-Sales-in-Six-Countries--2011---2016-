SUGGESTIONS/NEXT STEP

 

1.     Careful advertising - It is especially important to carefully market your product because advertising, if not done correctly, can sometimes have a negative impact on your business. You should make sure that it is done correctly by taking all aspects into consideration before settling on a particular marketing strategy.

2.     Understand your customers - As a Bike Company owner, you need to have a good understanding of your customers since they are the ones to whom you are selling your products. Be sure to provide each and every customer – no matter their age or social demographics – the perfect bike or service. It is all about making your customers happy and offering them the best service possible.

3.     Identifying your niche - Marketing specialists will tell you that, if you’re going to attract a healthy customer base, you have to identify your sweet spot: aka the niche market that exists at the intersection of consumer need and your particular skill set.
